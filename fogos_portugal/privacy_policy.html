Privacy Policy – Fogos Portugal

Fogos Portugal is committed to protecting your privacy. This app collects and processes data only as necessary to provide its core features, such as fire alerts, map display, and notifications.

Information We Collect

  • Location data (if you grant permission) to show your position on the map and send relevant alerts.
  • Device information for app functionality and analytics.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging token for push notifications.

How We Use Your Information

  • To display your location and nearby incidents on the map.
  • To send notifications about fire alerts and updates.
  • To improve app performance and user experience.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.
  • Data may be processed by trusted services (e.g., Firebase) to enable notifications and analytics.

Your Choices

  • You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.
  • You can opt out of notifications in the app or device settings.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
