Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Welcome to Air Quality Maps App. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

Air Quality Maps App is designed to display air quality maps and station information. We do not require you to create an account to use the app.

  • Location data: If you choose to use map or station location features, the app may request access to your device location. This is used only to show nearby air quality stations and map features.
  • Device and usage data: The app may collect non-personal technical data such as device model, operating system version, and app usage behavior for the purpose of improving the app experience.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide map and air quality station features.
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs.
  • To provide a better user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services to support maps, analytics, and location features. These services may collect information needed to provide their functionality.

Examples of possible third-party providers include:

  • Google Maps
  • Mapbox
  • Firebase or similar analytics platforms

Please refer to the privacy policies of these providers for more details on how they handle data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share data only if required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information collected by the app. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app’s support or developer contact information listed in the store listing.
